Overview of Topics

This guide covers the essential mathematical techniques needed for your HNC programme:

  • Core Algebra: The foundation for manipulating equations and formulas in all other topics.
  • Trigonometry: Essential for analyzing anything involving waves, angles, or rotation, from AC circuits to mechanical systems.
  • Calculus: The mathematics of change. Used to find rates of change (differentiation) and accumulations (integration), which are critical for optimization and dynamic systems.
  • Linear Methods: Techniques for solving systems of simultaneous equations, vital for circuit analysis and structural mechanics.
  • Statistics: Using data to understand processes, control quality, and make informed decisions.
  • Financial Economics: The methods used to evaluate the financial viability of engineering projects, such as calculating depreciation and investment returns.
